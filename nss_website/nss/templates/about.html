Welcome to the National Service Scheme (NSS) at AWH Engineering College

The National Service Scheme (NSS) at AWH Engineering College is a vibrant and dynamic platform dedicated to fostering a spirit of community service, leadership, and social responsibility among students. As part of a nationwide initiative under the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India, our unit actively engages in various outreach programs, awareness campaigns, environmental drives, and capacity-building initiatives.

Through NSS, students gain opportunities to develop their personalities by participating in activities that make a tangible difference in society.
